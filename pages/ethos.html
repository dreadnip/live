Ethos

A good website is...

Small

Actively fight the web obesity crisis. Avoid large, complex systems. Question every link, include, script or image.

Keeping your site small and fast makes it accessible to more people.

Open source

Only by sharing our efforts, ideas and work can we move forward.

Well documented

Code should be modifiable by someone that knows how to program but hasn't seen this particular codebase.

Accessible

Write semantic HTML. Follow the WCAG 2.0 guidelines.

Guidelines

Unfinished

The web is allowed to be raw and unfinished. Waiting until something is polished before showing it to the world is waste.
